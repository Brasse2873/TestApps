WCF Test Client (WcfTestClient.exe)
===================================

- Service properties/Debug
  * Parameters: 
     /client:<pathAndNameToClientApp>
     /clientArgs:<argumentsToBePassedToClientApp>


Example:/client:wcftestclient.exe /clientargs:"http://localhost:8733/"

Normal use:
"C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Visual Studio 12.0\Common7\IDE\Wcftestclient.exe" "http://localhost:8733/"
